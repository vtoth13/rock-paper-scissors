Rock Paper Scissors

Game Rules

Objective: The objective of Rock, Paper, Scissors is to defeat your opponent by choosing an item that beats their chosen item.

Items:
  1. Rock: Represented by a closed fist, rock crushes scissors.
  2. Paper: Represented by an open hand, paper covers rock.
  3. Scissors: Represented by a fist with the index and middle finger extended, scissors cut paper.
Winning and Losing
  • Rock crushes scissors, so if you choos rock and your opponent chooses scissors, you win.
  • Paper covers rock, so if you choose paper and your opponent chooses rock, you win.
  • Scissors cut paper, so if you choose scissors and your opponent chooses paper, you win.

Ties: If both players shoose the same item, the game is a tie, and it is usually played again to determine the winner.

How to Play:
  1. Select a move of your choice: rock, paper or scissors.
  2. The computer will randomly generate its choice for each round.
  3. The winner of each round is determined based on the interactionos between the chosen items.
  4. After 10 rounds, the game will display the final score and declare the player as the winner or loser.
